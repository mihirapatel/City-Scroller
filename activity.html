Activities

1. Shark's Ice Skating Rink
$$
 Address: 44388 Old Warm Springs Blvd, Fremont, CA 94538
Shark's Ice Skating Rink is the official location for the local hockey team, San Jose Sharks, to practice. It is an excellent place to come with friends and skate, and is open from 6 AM to 11 PM.
2. iFly
$$$$
 Address: 31310 Alvarado-Niles Rd, Union City, CA 94587
iFly is located in the Union Landing Shopping Center. It is open from 9 AM to 11 PM and is a great place to get a good workout and have fun.
3. City Beach
$$
 Address: 4020 Technology Pl, Fremont, CA 94538
City Beach is open from 11 AM to 10 PM. It is a recreational center with indoor rock climbing, an American eatery, and more!
4. Rockin' Jump
$$
 Address: 39177 Farwell Dr, Fremont, CA 94538
Rockin' Jump, located in the Mowry East Shopping Center, is a perfect place of fun to come with friends. Filled with trampolines, it is great to jump around and have a great time!
5. Laser Tag

 Address: 5759 Stevenson Blvd, Newark, CA 94560
Laser Tag Inc. is open from 12 PM to 9PM is a great place to come with a group of friends and play laser tag.

$$
 Address: 40645 Fremont Blvd, Fremont, CA 94538
