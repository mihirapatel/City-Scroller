Shopping Centers

1. Pacific Commons
$$
 Address: 43918 Boscell Rd, Fremont, CA 94538
Pacific Commons is a shopping center filled with a variety of stores perfect for shopping, including Nordstrom Rack, Old Navy, and Toys R' Us. Also, there is a plethora of food and desserts including Cold Stone Creamery.
2. Newpark Mall
$$
 Address: 2086 Newpark Mall, Newark, CA 94560
The Newpark Mall is the closest mall to Fremont, which has the AMC movie theater and a large selection of department and mall stores. It is the perfect place to hang out with friends and shop.
3. Fremont Hub
$$
 Address: 39100 Argonaut Way, Fremont, CA 94538
The Fremont Hub is the downtown of Fremont, having stores such as Ulta Beauty, Target, Trader Joes, and Nothing Budnt Cakes! It is a great place to walk around, eat, and shop.
4. Union Landing Shopping Center
$$
 Address: I-880, Union City, CA 94587
Union Landing is located in Union City, close to North Fremont, and contains a movie theater, iFly, Krispy Kreme, and a large variety of restaurants.
