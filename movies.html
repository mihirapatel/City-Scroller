Movie Theaters

1. AMC Newpark
$$$
 Address: 400 Newpark Mall, Newark, CA 94560
The AMC Newpark theatre is located in the NewPark Mall. It contains reserved seating, so you can buy and pick your seats online and do not have to come early to the movies because your seat will be reserved for you. The seats are adjustable and provide a comfortable environment for a range of movies.
2. Century Theatres at Pacific Commons
$$
 Address: 43917 Pacific Commons Blvd, Fremont, CA 94538
Century Theatres is located in the Pacific Commons shopping center. Unlike AMC, you do not have to reserve seats so it is perfect for spontaneous plans. However, if you are gong to a very popular movie it is important to come early to pick out good seats!
