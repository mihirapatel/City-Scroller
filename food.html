Food

1. Sala Thai
$$
 Address: 39170 State St, Fremont, CA 94538
Sala Thai is a local Thai food restaurant that is open on Monday-Thursday from 11 AM to 9:30 PM, Friday and Saturday from 11 AM to 10 PM, and Sunday from 12 PM to 9 PM. It has both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, and was voted the best Thai restaurant in 2005.
2. The Habit
$
 Address: 43830 Pacific Commons Blvd, Fremont, CA 94538
The Habit is open from 10:30 AM to 10 PM. It is a great burger chain with a range of options, including vegetarian and meat options.
3. Bombay Pizza House
$$
 Address: 4922 Paseo Padre Pkwy, Fremont, CA 94555
Bombay Pizza House is open from 11 AM to 10 PM. It is a fusion between Indian food and Italian food, having pizzas with Indian spices and Italian toppings.
4. La Pinata
$$
 Address: 39136 Paseo Padre Pkwy, Fremont, CA 94538
La Pinata is a Mexican food restaurant located in Gateway Plaza, open from 10 AM to 11 PM. It has scenic murals on the walls and a tequilla bar to create a destive atmosphere at this Mexican standby.
5. Le Papillon
$$$
 Address: 37296 Mission Blvd, Fremont, CA 94536
Papillon is a French restaurant and is open from 11:30 AM to 2 PM. It serves traditional French cuisine and wine served in a fromal, white-tablecloth dining room and covered atrium.
