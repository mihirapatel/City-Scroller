Outdoor Activities

1. Mission Peak
Free
 Address: Mission Peak, California 94539
Mission Peak is a scenic hike that has two trails that are fun to climb both at sunset and sunrise. It is a great place to get exercise but also go on adventures with friends.
2. Coyote Hills
Free
 Address: 8000 Patterson Ranch Rd, Fremont, CA 94555
Coyote Hills is a regional park with both bike trails and hiking trails, having a great view of part of the bay at the top. In addition, there are often community events in relation to native american tribes that aim to conserve their culture.
3. Don Edwards National Wildlife Refuge
Free
 Address: 2 Marshlands Rd, Fremont, CA 94555
Don Edwards National Wildlife Refuge is a national park that has a great view of the Bay. It is a great place to walk alone or with friends.
4. Lake Elizabeth
Free
 Address: 40000 Paseo Padre Pkwy, Fremont, CA 94538
Lake Elizabeth, or Central Park, is a park located in the middle of Fremont. It has parks, boating, and bike trails, perfect for a summer day.
